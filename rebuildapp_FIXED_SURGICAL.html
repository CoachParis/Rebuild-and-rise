Welcome to Rebuild & Rise.

I want to start by saying how excited I am to have you here. This program was created for the woman who is ready to move beyond just starting over. You've already begun the process of rebuilding — and now, this is where we rise. With more structure. More strength. More intention. And most importantly, more self-trust.

Inside this experience, you are not being asked to force, punish, or override your body. You are being invited to build in a way that is more supportive, more sustainable, and far more powerful over time.

So as you move through this program, let this be your reminder: you do not need to chase random workouts or all-or-nothing intensity to make progress. We build through repetition, structure, and the kind of consistency your body can actually trust.

Give yourself permission to move with intention. To learn your patterns. To honor the process. And to recognize that strength is not only something you perform — it is something you build.

I'm so glad you're here. Let's get started.
